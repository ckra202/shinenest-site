Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

ShineNest Shop ("we," "our," or "us") operates as an e-commerce business selling products through third-party marketplace platforms, including Amazon. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our business operations and internal tools.

Information We Collect

In the course of our business operations, we may access and process the following types of information:

  • Product catalog data (product titles, descriptions, categories, dimensions, and identifiers such as ASINs)
  • Product pricing and marketplace offer data
  • Fulfillment fee estimates and shipping-related data
  • Sales rank and product performance metrics

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) of Amazon buyers or customers. Our internal tools operate exclusively with product-level data.

How We Use Information

All data accessed through marketplace APIs is used solely for our own internal business operations, including:

  • Evaluating products for sourcing and resale decisions
  • Calculating profitability, margins, and fulfillment costs
  • Monitoring product performance and market conditions
  • Managing inventory and business operations

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, lease, or distribute any Amazon Information or marketplace data with any third parties. All data is used exclusively for our own internal business purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS
  • Secure credential storage using environment variables and encrypted key management
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and monitoring
  • Multi-factor authentication on all accounts

Data Retention

Product-level data is retained only for as long as it is necessary for our business operations. Data is securely deleted when it is no longer needed.

Third-Party Platforms

Our business operates on third-party marketplace platforms that have their own privacy policies. We encourage customers to review the privacy policies of those platforms for information about how they handle customer data.

Incident Response

In the event of a security incident involving marketplace data, we will notify the relevant platform (e.g., Amazon at security@amazon.com) within 24 hours of detection and take immediate steps to mitigate any impact.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, you can reach us through our seller profile on Amazon or by visiting this website.
